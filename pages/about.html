ABOUT THE DIVISION

Committed to Nature.
Dedicated to Conservation.

Chalakudy Forest Division is one of the ecologically rich and diverse regions of Kerala. The division is home to dense forests, rivers, waterfalls and protected wildlife habitats.

We work towards sustainable forest management, wildlife protection, anti-poaching, eco-restoration, tribal welfare and public awareness programs.
