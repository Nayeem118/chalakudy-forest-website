About Chalakudy Division

Chalakudy Division is one of the major forest divisions in Kerala, known for its evergreen forests, rich wildlife, and vibrant biodiversity.

The division is committed to conservation, protection and sustainable management of forest resources for the benefit of present and future generations.

The division plays a vital role in preserving ecological balance while working closely with local communities, stakeholders and institutions to promote environmental awareness.

Our Mission

To conserve and protect forest and wildlife, maintain ecological balance and ensure sustainable use of forest resources.

Our Vision

A society that values and protects forests and wildlife, ensuring ecological harmony and quality of life for all.

Our Values

  • Conservation First
  • Integrity & Transparency
  • Community Participation
  • Sustainable Development
